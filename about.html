About Us

Who we are ?

At SewaSankalConnect, we strongly believe in the transformative power of service and its pivotal role in nurturing thriving communities. Our mission is deeply rooted in the spirit of 'sewa' - selfless service to others. We are dedicated to fostering the development of society, uplifting youth welfare, and enhancing social well-being. Our commitment to making a tangible difference is reflected in our tireless efforts to create sustainable and impactful initiatives that bring about positive change. Join us in our journey of service and let's make a meaningful difference together.

What we do ?

SewaSankalConnect is committed to raising awareness among communities about government schemes and policies aimed at uplifting marginalized sections of society. Through our dedicated efforts, we strive to bridge the information gap and empower individuals with the knowledge they need to access essential resources and support. By fostering greater understanding and engagement, we aim to create a more inclusive and supportive environment for all members of society. Join us in our mission to build a more informed and empowered community.

How we do this?

At SewaSankalConnect, our mission extends beyond connecting volunteers with NGO initiatives. We are dedicated to addressing critical issues such as the prevention of road accidents, recognizing the vital role of community engagement in promoting road safety. Understanding the importance of holistic well-being, we have integrated programs focusing on both vitamin fortification and comprehensive road safety awareness. By fostering collaboration between volunteers and impactful NGO projects, we strive to create a safer and more secure environment for all members of society. Join us in our journey of service, where every contribution makes a significant difference in building a better, safer community for everyone.
